Gracie Davidson

Bio

Gracie is a current graduate student at George Mason University, pursuing a master's degree in English literature and a graduate certificate in publishing. She received her bachelor's degree in English literature with a minor in American Sign Language from GMU in May 2025, as a member of both the Honors College and the English Honors Program. During her undergraduate studies, she was the recipient of the Aileen Walls Award, the James L. Jackson Award, and the Innovation in Methods and Ideas Award for her Honors thesis paper; she also participated in several organizations, such as Poetry Daily, Book Buzz, the International English Honor Society. With a history of literary work and volunteer experiences, she plans to work in the publishing industry, specifically in an editorial or production role.
